Meet Our Founder

Rima
Beauty Expert

Certified Master Beautician • Hair Stylist & Beauty Artist
Founder of New Look Salon

Rima is a passionate beauty professional with over 15+ years of experience in the beauty and wellness industry. Her journey began with a small salon and a big dream — driven by creativity, hard work, and a deep passion for hairstyling and beauty.

Starting from humble beginnings, she moved forward with determination and dedication, transforming her vision into a trusted salon brand known for quality service and personalized beauty care. Her passion for helping women feel confident and beautiful continues to inspire her work every day.

Certified by Zoraanes Makeup Studio with a Diploma in Beautician, Rima also brings modern salon techniques to every client experience.

She is especially known for:

  • ◆ Creative Haircuts & Modern Hair Styling
  • ◆ Professional Makeup Services
  • ◆ Hair Treatments & Hair Care
  • ◆ Eyebrow Shaping & Skincare
  • ◆ Bridal Beauty & Complete Makeovers

Rima believes beauty is a form of confidence and self-expression. With a talented team of beauticians, she focuses on delivering luxury salon experiences tailored to every client's unique style and personality.
